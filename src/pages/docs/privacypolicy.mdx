# Privacy Policy

Thank you for using our services. As a responsible service provider, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and disclose your personal information in accordance with applicable laws and regulations.

## Collection and Use of Personal Data

We only collect and keep your real name, email address, and password for login purposes. We do not collect or store any personal data such as audio or media data from the devices you use.

## Storage and Cancellation of Personal Data

We store your personal data, including your real name, password, and address, solely for the purpose of providing our services to you. If you wish to cancel our services, you can do so by clicking the "cancel services" button located at the bottom of the account page. Once you submit the cancellation request, our system will automatically cancel your services. **Please note that we do not offer any refund policy.**

## Personal SBC and Host Data

We may keep your personal SBC (Single Board Computer) or any host data from the client software that you use for our services. This data is used only for the purpose of visualizing your personal profile and controlling your IoT (Internet of Things) devices. We take all reasonable steps to ensure that your personal data is secure and protected from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. We may disclose your personal data if required by law or if we believe that such disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, your safety, or the safety of others.

If you have any questions or concerns regarding our privacy policy, please contact us at support@roboreactor.com. We reserve the right to update this policy from time to time, and any changes will be posted on our website.
